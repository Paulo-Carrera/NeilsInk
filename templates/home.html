Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is your tattoo studio's location?

    We are located in the city of El Monte, California. More details will be available upon request.

  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by visiting our Schedule Now page. After scheduling, you will receive a confirmation email. If you have any questions or need more information, please don't hesitate to contact us on our Contact Us page.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    You can pay for your deposit via cash, CashApp, Venmo, or PayPal in person or online through the Schedule Now page.

  • What are your working hours?

    Availability varies depending on your location and the desired service. We are flexible and are open to any time that is convenient for you. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us on our Contact Us page.

  • What is your deposit policy?

    We require a deposit of $15.00, which you can pay upon scheduling your appointment on the Schedule Now page, or in person at the studio.

  • Can I get an estimate for my desired service?

    Absolutely! Just send us a message on our Contact Us page, and we will provide you with a free estimate.

  • Do you do walk-ins?

    We do not offer walk-in appointments. Please schedule your appointment on our Schedule Now page to avoid any inconvenience.
