Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is your tattoo studio's location?

    We are located in the city of El Monte, California. More details will be available upon request.

  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by visiting our Schedule Now page. After scheduling, you will receive a confirmation email. If you have any questions or need more information, please don't hesitate to contact us on our Contact Us page.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    You can pay for your deposit via cash, CashApp, Venmo, or PayPal in person or online through the Schedule Now page.

  • What are your working hours?

    Availability varies depending on your location and the desired service. We are flexible and are open to any time that is convenient for you. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us on our Contact Us page.

  • What is your deposit policy?

    We require a deposit of $15.00, which you can pay upon scheduling your appointment on the Schedule Now page, or in person at the studio.

  • Can I get an estimate for my desired service?

    Absolutely! Just send us a message on our Contact Us page, and we will provide you with a free estimate.

  • Do you do walk-ins?

    We do not offer walk-in appointments. Please schedule your appointment on our Schedule Now page to avoid any inconvenience.

Why choose NeilsInk?

    Your safety is our top priority. Our studio follows strict hygiene protocols that exceed industry standards to ensure your experience is as safe as it is comfortable. From daily deep cleaning of our facilities to the use of disposable gloves and single-use tools, every measure is taken to protect our clients and staff. Additionally, all equipment is sterilized with state-of-the-art techniques before and after each use, so you can have complete confidence in the cleanliness of our environment.

    We understand that getting a tattoo or piercing is a personal journey, and we respect your privacy every step of the way. Our private, comfortable spaces are designed to ensure that you feel at ease while sharing your ideas and undergoing your procedure. Rest assured, any personal information you share with us remains confidential and is used solely to provide the best service possible. Your trust is invaluable to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

  • Quality

    Excellence is at the heart of everything we do. From the moment you walk into NeilsInk, you’ll notice the difference in the quality of our equipment, materials, and service. We source only the finest inks and tools to ensure vibrant, long-lasting results. Here, we stay up-to-date in the latest techniques and trends to deliver precision and artistry in every design. With NeilsInk, you can trust that your tattoo or piercing will not only meet but exceed your expectations.
